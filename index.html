Kent S Johnson

Professional software developer and data analyst with many years experience. Committed to using my powers for good.

Professional Work

In my day job I write software to acquire and analyze tissue images used for cancer research.

Multispectral Imaging

Multispectral imaging of stained tissue samples allows researchers to see detailed relationships between tumor cells and their environment.

Spatial Analysis

Spatial analysis shows touching cells, giving insight to inter-cellular interactions in the tumor environment.

Boston West Fair Skies

I am on the steering committee of Boston West Fair Skies, a citizen activist group working for a fairer distribution of airplane noise from Boston Logan Airport. My analysis and visualizations have helped move the the conversation with Massport and the FAA from stone-walling to engagement.

Flight Paths from Logan 33L

This map shows the change in distribution of flight paths from 2013 to 2015. A version of this map was on the front page of the Boston Globe!

Noise Measurement

Volunteers measure aircraft noise levels. Analysis of the noise levels shows the inadequacy of current noise metrics.

Cambridge Civic Data

I work with Cambridge civic groups to map data from the Cambridge Open Data portal.

Cambridge Tree Map

Street trees in the Cambridge database.

Cambridge Lighting Zones

Zones in a proposed Cambridge lighting ordinance.

Alewife Development

Current, planned and expected development in the Alewife region of Cambridge.

Accessory Housing
